Shady Oak's Activities

Here's a list of activities you can look forward to while staying with us:

- Swimming pool (9:00 AM - Dusk)
- Horseshoes
- Children's play area with swings, slide, playhouse and climber.
- Saturday morning Crafts For Kids
- Recreation hall with activities posted daily
- Card games
- Pot luck meals (Brunch or dinner)
- Ice cream socials
- Cook off contests
- ... and don't forget the train!

Join us Sunday mornings for our pancake breakfast featuring
Wild Maine Blueberry Sauce and freshly brewed coffee.

Keep an eye on the activities board so you don't miss any of the fun!
