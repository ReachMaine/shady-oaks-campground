Shady Oak's Trailer

Try our modern trailer for a cozy camping alternative. Sleeps up to 4 people with one queen bed and one fold out full couch. Bring your sleeping bags or standard bedding and gear. 3 Night minimum stay. Sorry, no pets allowed in trailer.
