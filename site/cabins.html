Shady Oak's Log Cabins

Try one of our log cabins for cozy camping comfort. Bring your sleeping bags or standard bedding and gear. Sorry, no pets allowed in cabins.

Our cabins are 12' x 16', feature 2 double bunk beds with foam mattress, electricity, refrigerator, small table, covered porch, outside picnic table and fire ring...

...a comfortable step up from tenting!
