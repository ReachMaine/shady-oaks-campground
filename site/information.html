Campground Information

In order to make your stay with us more pleasant, we ask that the following policies be observed by campers and their guests. Thank you for staying at Shady Oaks!

David & Marlene Greenlaw

Policies & Reminders

  • Check in 1:00 pm. Check out 11:00 am.
  • Quiet time 10:00 pm to 8:00 am and is strictly enforced.
  • Speed limit is 5 mph and is strictly enforced.
  • All guests must check in and register at the office.
    Guest fees:
    • Daily rate $3 per person.
    • Overnight rate $5 per person.
  • The use of firearms and fireworks is prohibited.
  • Bicycles are not to be ridden on walkways or after dark.
  • Mini bikes and ATV's are not permitted.
  • If you wish to extend your stay, please notify the office.

Security Gate

  • All residents are asked to maintain the integrity of the security gate system by not giving or loaning your automatic control to anyone. This service is for residents use only. If you are expecting guests, please notify the office.

Camp Site

  • One RV and two motor vehicles per site.
  • Sewer hookups:  Your RV sewer lines must be accompanied with an approved hose/connection system to provide a tight fit to eliminate gas fumes.  Waste water cannot be emptied on the ground (State Law.)
  • Residents shall maintain their space in a clean and orderly manner.
  • No outside clotheslines permitted on site.
  • Improvements to your site must be approved by management.
  • Storage units must be approved by management.
  • Campfires are permitted in the provided fire rings only. Please do not relocate fire rings.
  • Trees should not be cut or otherwise damaged.

Children

  • Parents are responsible for the actions and safety of their children at all times.
  • Children under 14 are not allowed in the swimming pool without a supervising adult.
  • Young children must be supervised by an adult while using the playground.
  • Bicycles are not to be ridden on walkways or after dark.

Guests

  • All guests must check in and register at the office.
    Guest fees:
    •  Daily rate $3 per person.
    • Overnight rate $5 per person.
  • Tenants must be on the premises and are responsible for guests.
  • Parents are responsible for the actions and safety of their children at all times.
  • Limit of 4 guests per campsite.

Dogs

  • Dogs are allowed ONLY in The Estates and in the designated dog park.
  • Dogs must be accompanied by owner at all times.
  • Dogs must be on leash.
  • No chained dogs are allowed.
  • Owners are responsible for cleaning up after their dog(s).

Laundry

  • Laundry is available 24 hours (at the Bath House and in The Estates.)
  • Do not leave clothes unattended.
  • Dying clothes is not permitted.

Trash

  • All trash must be placed in the dumpster. All boxes must be broken down.
  • Burning trash is not allowed.
  • Seafood shells must be placed in plastic bags and then disposed of.

Pool

  • Hours 9:00 am to Dusk.
  • Please note pool rules located within pool area.
  • Children under 14 are not allowed in the swimming pool area without a supervising adult.

Smoking

  • All buildings (including cabins, store, recreation hall, bath house and laundry rooms) the pool and the porch area are smoke free.
  • Please do not throw your butts on the ground.

Mail

  • When having mail sent to Shady Oaks, please use this format:
    Your Name
    32 Leaches Point Road
    Your site number
    Orland, ME 04472

Emergency

  • In case of emergency, dial 911 and then call the office 207. 469. 7739.
  • Emergency phone messages will be delivered immediately.
  • Please notify management of all accidents, emergencies or of any unsafe conditions.
