Welcome!

Shady Oaks is a 70 site campground with 5 rental cabins. The majority of our sites are full hookup with 30/50 amp electric, water and sewer. We have restrooms and free showers. We have an onsite laundry room, dump station and free Wi-Fi. Our store carries some food items and RV supplies. We also offer LP gas and firewood for those nightly campfires.

Our recreational facilities include a swimming pool, horseshoe pit, basketball hoop, play house, swings and slide. The recreation hall is a gathering place for ice cream socials, pot luck dinners, arts and crafts for the all ages, cards and games.

Wherever your destination, you're certain to find your stay at Shady Oaks Campground relaxing and enjoyable. We welcome travelers, vacationers and seasonal campers. And now, we welcome Pets in designated area.
